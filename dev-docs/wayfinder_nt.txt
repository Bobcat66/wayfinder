Wayfinder uses a special NT Table (wayfinder/control) to send and receive control messages. The control table
has the following topics:

WFTS (bool): This is published by the RoboRIO, and signals to coprocessors whether or not the RoboRIO is acting as a WFTS server

SLAMNode (string): This is published by the RoboRIO, and signals to the coprocessors which one of them is to act as the SLAM node.
The RoboRIO can also leave it empty and not assign a SLAM server
